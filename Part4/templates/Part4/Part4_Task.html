PART 4


Each bin represents a certain quantity (in steps of 10) of Green balls you (Box A) and your teammate (Box B) received in Part 1. Each token represents one percentage point. Therefore, the number of tokens you distribute to each bin in Box A represents your belief about the percent chance that you received a certain quantity of Green balls. The number of tokens you distribute to each bin in Box B represents your belief about the percent chance that your teammate received a certain quantity of Green balls. The more likely you deem a certain bin to contain the number of Green balls you and your teammate received, the more tokens should be put into this bin.

The task will only be finished when you have distributed exactly 100 tokens into the 10 bins in each of Box A and Box B.
